Privacy Policy

Effective: March 22, 2026

Manifest is a document signature workflow platform, part of the DockLabs suite. This policy supplements the DockLabs Privacy Policy, which governs all DockLabs services. Where this policy is silent, the DockLabs Privacy Policy applies.

1. Data Collected by Manifest

In addition to the general data described in the DockLabs Privacy Policy, Manifest collects and processes: documents submitted for signature, signer information, signature records, and audit trails.

2. AI Processing

Manifest includes no AI features at this time. AI outputs are advisory and should be reviewed by qualified personnel before action is taken. Data processed by AI features is not used to train models.

3. Data Retention

Data retention in Manifest is configurable by your organization's administrator, with options for standard (7-year), extended (10-year), and permanent retention. Retention settings should align with your organization's records retention schedule and applicable legal requirements.

4. Security

Manifest inherits all security measures from the Keel platform, including HTTPS encryption, Content Security Policy headers, audit logging, role-based access controls, and file scanning. Access is managed through Microsoft Entra ID single sign-on.

5. Contact

For privacy questions specific to Manifest, contact Daniel O'Keefe at dokeefect.com.
