Executive Dashboard

Helm

Unified command dashboard for agency leadership. Aggregates real-time data from every DockLabs product into a single view. Cross-product bookmarks, cached feed snapshots refreshed on a configurable interval, morning briefings with severity thresholds and per-user preferences, and a live activity feed across the entire suite.

CRM for Economic Development

Beacon

FOIA-aware CRM for state economic development agencies. Three-zone architecture separates shared, state-internal, and partner-private data. Includes contact and company management with duplicate detection, interaction and pipeline tracking, adoption dashboards, and FOIA export to Admiralty.

Built for CT DECD and AdvanceCT.

Live Demo
Django 5.2 PostgreSQL htmx Bootstrap 5 DRF
Grants Management

Harbor

Full-lifecycle grants management from opportunity posting through closeout. AI-powered grant matching, 7-role RBAC, interactive map view, analytics dashboard, document signing with DocuSign integration, federal intake from Bounty, and FOIA export to Admiralty. English/Spanish i18n.

Live Demo GitHub
Django 5.2 PostgreSQL Claude AI Mapbox htmx Bootstrap 5 DocuSign
Document Signing

Manifest

Document signature workflows for grants and contracts. Template builder wizard, PDF placement editor, three signature methods plus DocuSign integration, and full audit trails. A standalone product that also integrates as a module of Harbor.

Live Demo
Django 5.2 PostgreSQL htmx Bootstrap 5
Federal Funds Discovery

Bounty

AI-powered federal grant discovery that syncs opportunities from Grants.gov and scores them against configurable focus areas using Claude AI. Self-registration with admin approval, opportunity tracking and collaboration, scheduled digest emails, and downstream push of won awards to Harbor. FOIA-aware via Keel.

FOIA Workflow

Admiralty

End-to-end FOIA request management for government agencies. Tracks requests from intake through response, catalogs statutory exemptions, and uses Claude AI to flag potential misclassifications during record review. Ingests FOIA-exportable records from Beacon, Harbor, Lookout, and Bounty. A standalone product but also a module of Beacon.
